More info about tabling

NiCE club tables nearly every weekday on Sproul Plaza. Our table is dressed in the green tablecloth and we usually have a variety of nuclear related items on display. In the above picture we had a collection of radiation sources used to calibrate detection machinery along with some natural sources of radiation (the bananas).
