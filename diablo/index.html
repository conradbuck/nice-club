Rules for Diablo Canyon Tour

Identification Requirements

If my tour includes the Protected Area, I understand that I will be required to show a valid government issued ID (an expired ID is not considered valid). Acceptable forms of identification include US driver's license, passport, DMV issued ID, or military ID.

Clothing Requirements

I understand that the following clothing is required:

  • Pants
  • Shirts with short or long sleeves (shoulders covered)
  • Shoes or boots that have a hard, flat sole, leather uppers, and both closed toes and closed heels. Heels must be less than 1.5 inches tall.
  • Visitor badge (provided)
  • Hard hats, ear protection, and safety glasses/gloves (provided) at some locations

Prohibited Items on Diablo Canyon Property

Upon entering Diablo Canyon Property, I understand that the following items are prohibited from my vehicle or on my person:

  • Firearms
  • Alcohol
  • Explosives
  • Illegal Narcotics
  • Incendiary Devices
  • Ammunition
  • Kombucha-Type Drinks
  • Medications Not in Original Packaging
  • Weapons, including knives or pocketknives

NO PHOTOS ALLOWED

Do not take pictures with your phone, you will have problems if you do
