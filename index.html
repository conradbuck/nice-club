NiCE Club @ UC Berkeley

Nuclear is Clean Energy N.i.C.E.

Founded in 2023 by Grant Mills M.S., NiCE club is the leading student voice for pro-nuclear advocacy on the UC Berkeley Campus. Our meetings engage with a wide variety of activities from advocacy and speaker events, extending to club socials and industry field trips.
